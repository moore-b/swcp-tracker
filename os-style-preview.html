Ordnance Survey maps are the gold standard for UK hiking - they show terrain, contours, footpaths, and landmarks clearly. These alternatives provide similar styling without the detail overload.

Best for: Trail navigation
Features: Terrain, paths, OS-inspired design
3. CartoDB Positron (Clean)
Style: Clean, minimal
Best for: Clear navigation
Features: Less detail, good contrast
4. Stamen Terrain
Style: Terrain-focused
Best for: Landscape appreciation
